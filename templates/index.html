The Problem

I source my fabrics from second hand places (i.e. Thirft Stores, FB Marketplace, Estate Sales) that come in all shapes, sizes, and patterns. I have sewing patterns that can be made with fabric for people that come in all shapes and sizes. And I have friends that come in all shapes and sizes (that I do not know exactly). But what patterns can I make with which fabric for what friend?

The Solution

Create an overkill application that does the math for me!

For my Friends:

Login and enter your basic measurements.

For Me:

I log my pattern library and fabric stash dimensions.

Through the magic of computer programming, this app cross-references everything to tell me exactly what I can make for you. Kinda cool, right???
